FAQs

For Businesses Searching for a Web Design or Development Company

What services do you offer for startups looking to establish an online presence?

We provide end-to-end web design and development—from UX/UI and wireframes to custom WordPress, Shopify, and CMS-based builds. We also offer training, maintenance, and visual refreshes.

What’s the difference between web design and web development?

Web design focuses on the layout, branding, and user journey; development involves coding and functionality. As a full-service web development agency in Singapore, we cover both.

Why choose Webdorks?

Webdorks has team members based in Singapore, Malaysia, and Vietnam, allowing us to support businesses across the region more effectively.

Our team can communicate in English, Chinese, Malay, Burmese, and Vietnamese, making collaboration smoother for clients with regional stakeholders and multilingual requirements upon kick-off.

Do you build custom websites or use templates?

We tailor each project to your needs. Whether it’s a fast-loading custom-coded site or a flexible CMS, we’ll recommend the best approach.

How long does it take to launch a typical corporate website?

Most projects take between 4 to 8 weeks, depending on complexity, feedback turnaround, and content readiness.

As a general guide:

  • Small websites: 4–6 weeks

  • Medium websites: 8–12 weeks

  • Large or complex websites: 12–20 weeks (depending on scope)

For Businesses Looking for Ecommerce Websites

What ecommerce platforms do you work with?

We specialize in Shopify and WooCommerce, both scalable and mobile-friendly. We can also assist with platform migration or redesigns.

Can you integrate local payment gateways?

Yes. We support PayPal, Stripe, PayNow, HitPay, and other SG-friendly payment options, with checkout flow optimization.

Will my ecommerce website be mobile-optimized?

Absolutely. Every site we build includes responsive layouts and mobile-first UX principles—crucial for Singapore’s mobile-savvy shoppers.

Do you handle product imports, categories, and filters?

Yes. We’ll help you organize product data, set up categories, filters, and tagging—so customers can shop with ease.

How fast can you launch an ecommerce website?

Typically, a mid-sized e-commerce store takes 8-12 weeks to complete.
Faster timelines may be possible if product information, branding assets, and content are ready from the start.

As a guide:

  • Small store setups: 4-8 weeks

  • Mid-sized stores: 8-12 weeks

  • Large or custom-functionality stores: 12-16+ weeks (depending on scope)

For Startups & Teams Needing UI/UX SaaS Prototyping

What is UI/UX prototyping, and why is it important for SaaS?

UI/UX prototyping allows you to visualize your product before development. It saves time, reduces risk, and ensures your platform meets real user needs from the start.

Do you help with MVP design for startups?

Yes. We specialize in MVP UI/UX design for SaaS—mapping core user flows, streamlining screens, and preparing developer-ready mockups.

What tools do you use for prototyping?

We use Figma for high-fidelity UI design and clickable prototypes. Tools like Miro or FigJam are used for flow planning and workshops.

How many screens are included in a typical prototype?

That depends on your user journey. Most MVPs include 10–20 screens. We advise based on scope and help reduce unnecessary complexity.

Can you hand over the designs to our internal or external dev team?

Yes. All designs are provided in Figma with component specs, interaction notes, and version control for smooth developer collaboration.

For Businesses Exploring Custom Web App Development

What qualifies as a custom web application?

A custom web app is a browser-based software solution tailored to your unique workflows—like booking systems, dashboards, CRMs, or internal tools—not just a regular content-based website.

How long does it take to build a custom web app?

Depending on the scope, most MVP-level web apps take 8–12+ weeks. Complex apps with integrations and admin panels may take 3–6+ months.

Do you offer both design and development for web apps?

Yes. We provide full UI/UX design in Figma, followed by agile development using modern stacks like React, Next.js, Laravel, and Node.js.

Can you integrate external platforms like payment gateways or CRMs?

Absolutely. We integrate tools & APIs like Stripe, Xero, HubSpot, FedEx, Google APIs, and more to connect your app with essential services.

Why choose an agency when AI can do the work?

AI tools can help accelerate prototyping and early-stage development. However, building a production-ready application still requires clear workflows, the right system architecture, integrations, testing, security considerations, and ongoing maintenance.

Webdorks helps turn your business requirements into a structured, scalable, and maintainable product. We use AI where it improves speed and efficiency, while our team manages the higher-stakes parts of the build from UI/UX and development to deployment and support.
